The 4 founders of Hogwarts

Hogwarts was founded by 4 people who were...

  • Godric Gryffindor
  • Salazar Slytherin
  • Rowena Ravenclaw
  • Helega Hufflepuff

    • Godric Gryffindor was an extremely well known duelist.He was holding a grudge against goblins.He was the founder of Gryffindor house and wanted his students to be brave like him.He gave his sword to the school.

    Salazaar Slytherin was known for being a Parselmouth.It means he could speak to snakes.He hated all Muggleborns & called them Mudbloods.He was the founder of Slytherin house and wanted his students to be very ambitious.He gave his locket to the school.

    Rowena Ravenclaw was known for her smartness and wit.She was the founder of Ravenclaw house and wanted her students to have the most wit like her.She gave her diadem to the school.

    Helega Hufflepuff was known for.She was the founder of Hufflepuff house and wanted her students to be artistic.She also accepted students who nobody else wanted.She gave her cup to the school.

    All of them got on very well.Salazaar Slytherin wished to be more selective by selecting students from pure-blood families.As the other founders did not agree , he left the school.But before he left, he built a hidden chamber that only his true heir can open.
